Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-03

This privacy policy describes how Audient handles information when you use the app.

Capture Features

Audient uses microphone, camera, and photo library access only when you choose capture features.

Feedback and Supabase

You may submit feedback voluntarily. If submitted, feedback may be stored in Supabase.

Optional Email

An optional email address may be collected only if you choose to enter it.

Practice Reminders

Practice reminders are local notifications on your device.

Data Sales

Audient does not sell personal data.

Local Storage of Captured Ideas

Captured ideas are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share or export them.
